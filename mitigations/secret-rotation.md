# Secret Rotation

Secret rotation replaces credentials that may have been exposed or reachable through a vulnerability. This page contains generic public guidance only.

Public-safe guidance:

- Rotate exposed keys, tokens, passwords, and certificates.
- Revoke old credentials when supported.
- Review public vendor documentation for product-specific rotation steps.
- Avoid recording actual credential values, account identifiers, or private environment details in this wiki.
